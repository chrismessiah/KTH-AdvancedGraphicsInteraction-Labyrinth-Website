A maze inside a cylinder in VR set in space with black hole portals and a 80’s retro theme.

That's one heck of a tagline. This project by 6 students in 2 months in Advanced Graphics and Interaction course at KTH, Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden. We chose to rethink the classic maze-game and create a modern interpretation suited for VR.

Challenges, Obstacles and Conclusions

  • We had to rewrite the movement controller to take into account the inverted gravity.
  • The mapping of a flat surface to a cylindrical with deformed wall blocks took a lot if time to work out
  • Cyber sickness was a major challenge and we made several attempts to counter.
  • The procedural maze generation was challenging but we managed to get it working.
  • The procedural mazes were very difficult to play with and we had to figure out how to make them easier.
  • Our limited access to hardware meant we had to plan a lot in advance for the interaction sessions. For project management Trello helped us out a lot.
  • Git and Unity is a nightmare. Never have people working in the same scenes. Make sure that you then merge everything on one computer while the whole team is watching. Avoid 4K resolutions unless you want the Git repo to become an non-convering limit, the git history will scale to infinity and you will need to create a new repo.
